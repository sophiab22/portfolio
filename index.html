a little bit about me

Currently I am an undergrad student in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. I am studying design and print and digital publishing. Through hard work and by being a little detailed oriented, I aim to conceptualize ideas into substantial products, no matter how crazy it is.

When I am not focusing all my energy on a project, I enjoy sketching random ideas, photography and relaxing with good music.
